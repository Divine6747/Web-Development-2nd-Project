Our Accomodation

All rooms are ensuite with tea/coffee making facilities and TVs. All rooms over look our large walled garden. We also offer bedrooms on the ground floor with their own private entrance. There are two guest lounges exclusively for guest use. Free private car park is provided at the Gardens, also WiFi in public areas.
